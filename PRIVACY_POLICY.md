# Privacy Policy for Otachaki

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025

## Overview

Otachaki is a Go (game) position evaluation training application. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**Otachaki does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

### What Data is Stored Locally

The app stores the following data **only on your device**:
- Your training statistics (accuracy, time spent)
- Your configuration preferences
- Custom dataset settings

This data:
- ✅ Never leaves your device
- ✅ Is not transmitted to any server
- ✅ Is not shared with third parties
- ✅ Is stored using standard Android/browser storage mechanisms

### Network Access

The app **does not require internet access** and works completely offline. The web version is hosted on GitHub Pages but stores all data in your browser's local storage.

## Third-Party Services

Otachaki does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Retention

All data remains on your device until:
- You uninstall the app (Android)
- You clear browser data (Web version)
- You manually reset statistics within the app

## Children's Privacy

Otachaki does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact:
- GitHub: https://github.com/siasio
- Repository: https://github.com/siasio/otachaki

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our servers.
