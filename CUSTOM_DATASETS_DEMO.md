- After creation, your new custom dataset will be automatically selected
- The **Dataset Settings** section will show configuration options for your custom dataset:
  - **Button Type** (Winner prediction, Rough lead, Exact score)
  - **Time per Problem**
  - **Position Type** (for final datasets)
  - **Ownership Display Mode**
  - And many more options!

- All changes are **saved automatically**
